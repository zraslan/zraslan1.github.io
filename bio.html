Biography

Hello,

Thank you for taking the time to visit my portfolio.

I am a Geospatial Analyst with a specialization in interdisciplinary research and applied GIS. I created this portfolio to showcase examples of GIS projects I am or have been a part of, ranging from LLD Telecommunication Drafting to complex modeling for advanced spatial analysis. I have a diverse professional background in GIS which includes working with the Connecticut State Data Center/Map and Geographic Information Center, University of Connecticut’s Geomorphology and Earth Processes Research Team, Wesleyan University’s Critical Health and Social Ecology Lab, KLJ Engineering’s Telecommunication Division, and Massachusetts Development Finance Agency.

In addition, I have been offering GIS and Digital Humanities tutoring services since 2013, founded a small Geospatial Consulting business (ClioGraphica) in 2015, and have been volunteering as a Mapper for the Humanitarian OpenStreetMap Team (HOT) since Spring 2023.

This overview of GIS by ESRI outlines how GIS is used, how it works, and offers a variety industry specific examples.

"Exploring With GIS" is a mini-series produced by ESRI in collaboration National Geographic.
